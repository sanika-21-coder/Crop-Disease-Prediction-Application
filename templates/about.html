About CropScan AI

This application was developed as part of our **PBL Semester 6** Project. Our goal is to leverage Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence to solve real-world agricultural problems.

How it Works

We trained a Convolutional Neural Network (MobileNetV2) on the PlantVillage Augmented Dataset containing over 38 different classes of crops and their diseases. The model analyzes the visual patterns of the plant leaves to determine its health status.

Features

  • 97%+ Accuracy using Transfer Learning.
  • Instant Context-Aware Chatbot Assistance.
  • Beautiful UI with Glassmorphism and Custom Glow Cursors.
  • Detailed Reports for Fertilizers and Solutions.
